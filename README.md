# Spotify Romaji Lyrics

### **Pronunciation for Japanese lyrics on Spotify.**

This extension automatically detects **Japanese lyrics** on Spotify and injects **Romaji subtitles** directly underneath each line.

## 🚀 **Features**

* **Dual-Language Display:** See original Japanese text with Romaji subtitles simultaneously.
* **Instant Conversion:** Powered by `kuroshiro` for fast and accurate romanization.

## 🛠️ **Installation**

### **1. Download the Extension**
1. Go to the [latest release](https://github.com/ntw-cts/Spotify-Romaji-Lyrics/releases/latest) page.
2. Download the ZIP file and extract it to a folder on your computer.

### **2. Load into Your Browser**
Choose your browser:
* **Google Chrome:** Type `chrome://extensions` in your search bar.
* **Microsoft Edge:** Type `edge://extensions` in your search bar.

**Next:**
1. Turn on **Developer mode** (a toggle usually located in the top right or bottom left corner of the page).
2. Click the **Load unpacked** button.
3. Select the **`dist`** folder located inside the folder you just extracted.

## 📝 **Usage**

1. **Open the Spotify Web Player** and play a Japanese song.
2. Click on the **Lyrics** (microphone icon) to view the lyrics page.
3. Click the **Spotify Romaji Lyrics** icon in your browser's extension toolbar.
4. Toggle **Enable Romaji** to **"ON"**.
